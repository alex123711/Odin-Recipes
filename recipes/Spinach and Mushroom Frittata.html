Spinach and Mushroom Frittata

Description

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 ½ tablespoons heavy whipping cream
  • 1 cup coarsely chopped spinach leaves
  • ½ cup diced mushrooms
  • ¼ cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  1. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Spray a 4 1/2-inch ramekin with nonstick spray.
  2. Whisk eggs and cream together in a bowl. Stir in spinach, mushrooms, Cheddar cheese, salt, and pepper until well combined. Pour into the prepared ramekin and cover tightly with foil.
  3. Air-fry for 12 minutes. Remove foil and air-fry until top starts to brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 4 to 8 more minutes.
